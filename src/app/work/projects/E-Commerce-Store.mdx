## Project Overview

I collaborated with **Zoots Cannabis Co.** to create a professional and visually engaging website that showcases their brand and enables seamless online shopping. Built using **WordPress** and **Elementor**, this site was designed to highlight their unique cannabis products, with **WooCommerce** powering the e-commerce functionality for product listings and customer transactions.

## Technologies Used

- **WordPress**: A robust content management system was chosen for its flexibility and ease of use, enabling Zoots to manage content updates effortlessly.
- **Elementor**: This powerful page builder was utilized to create a visually appealing and responsive layout, allowing for precise control over design and customization.
- **WooCommerce**: Integrated for e-commerce functionality, WooCommerce enabled efficient product listing, inventory management, and a smooth checkout process, tailored to meet industry standards and Zoots' specific requirements.

## Key Features

- **Custom Product Pages**: Leveraged WooCommerce to design customized product pages with detailed descriptions, images, and categorization, enhancing user experience and making it easy for customers to browse products.
- **Responsive Design**: Built with Elementor, the site is fully responsive across devices, ensuring an optimal user experience on both desktop and mobile.
- **Seamless Checkout**: Implemented WooCommerce to handle secure transactions, with streamlined checkout flows that offer customers a straightforward purchasing experience.
- **Easy Content Management**: WordPress provides Zoots with a user-friendly backend to update products, manage inventory, and edit content as needed without technical barriers.

## Challenges and Learnings

The primary challenges were **customizing WooCommerce** to fit Zoots' branding and product structure and ensuring **optimal performance** across all device types. Elementor's design flexibility helped overcome styling limitations, while WooCommerce extensions were tailored to meet the specific e-commerce needs of Zoots.

## Outcome

The website effectively showcases Zoots Cannabis Co.'s product line, providing customers with an engaging, intuitive shopping experience.
